Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Arro ("the app") handles user data. By using the app, you agree to this policy.

Data We Collect

Arro does not directly collect any personal data from you. However, the app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Through AdMob, the following data may be collected:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Device model and operating system version
  • Approximate location (IP-based, if permission is granted)
  • Ad impression and click data

This data is processed by Google. For details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Stored Locally

The following information is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any server:

  • Completed levels and game progress
  • Sound, music, vibration, and theme preferences
  • Language selection

Ad Preferences

On iOS 14 and later, the app may request permission through Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework for ad personalization. If you decline, non-personalized ads will continue to be shown. You can manage your preferences at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.

Children's Privacy

The app does not collect personal data from children under the age of 13. We recommend that children use the app under parental supervision.

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version is always accessible within the app. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.
