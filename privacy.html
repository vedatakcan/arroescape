Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how Vedat Akcan ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use the Arro Escape mobile application ("the App"). By downloading, installing, or using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

We are committed to minimizing the data we collect. We do not directly collect, store, or process any personal identification information (such as your name, email address, or phone number).

However, to provide our services and display advertisements, the App utilizes third-party services, specifically Google AdMob, which may collect the following information automatically:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers (e.g., IDFA for iOS, AAID for Android).
  • Usage Data: Ad interaction metrics (e.g., ad impressions, clicks).
  • Location Data: Approximate, IP-based location (only if device-level permissions allow).

2. Local Data Storage

To ensure a seamless user experience, the App stores certain data strictly locally on your device. This data is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Game progress, including unlocked levels and scores.
  • In-game settings, such as sound, music, vibration, and visual themes.
  • Selected language preferences.

If you uninstall the App or clear the App's data from your device settings, this local data will be permanently deleted.

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements within the App. Google may use advertising cookies and device identifiers to serve ads based on your prior usage of this and other apps.

For more details on how Google processes your data, please review the Google Privacy Policy.

4. Your Choices and Ad Preferences

You have control over the data collected for advertising purposes:

  • iOS Users (iOS 14+): The App utilizes Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You can choose whether to allow the App to track your activity for personalized advertising. You can update this preference anytime via your device settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking).
  • Android Users: You can opt out of personalized ads by navigating to your device settings (Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization).

5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date." We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
